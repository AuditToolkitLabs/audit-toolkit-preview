Audit Admin Toolkit

Operator-focused audit workflow platform covering findings, evidence management, scheduled audits, and reporting across mixed on-premises and hybrid environments.

View Licensing → Documentation Latest Release View Screenshots

Features

Audit workflow from detection to closure

A structured workflow platform designed around the needs of infrastructure audit teams — findings, evidence, reports, schedules, and administration in one place.

📊

Dashboard & Overview

Central audit health dashboard showing open findings, scheduled audits, recent activity, risk trends, and compliance status at a glance.

🔍

Audit Console & Findings

Structured findings workflow from identification through evidence capture, risk rating, owner assignment, remediation tracking, and verified closure.

📄

Reporting & Evidence Export

Evidence-oriented PDF and CSV reporting with on-demand and scheduled generation. Audit trail exports for compliance review and management reporting.

📅

Scheduled Audit Automation

Define, schedule, and automate recurring audit runs across target hosts. Integration with the managed agent for network-isolated segments.

👤

User & Role Management

Auditor, operator, and read-only role tiers with per-workspace access control. SSO integration and TOTP MFA supported.

⚙

Settings & Administration

Centralised configuration for integrations, notification channels, audit templates, report branding, and system health monitoring.
