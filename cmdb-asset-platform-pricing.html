Licensing & Pricing

Asset Command Center

Commercial model: one free evaluation route plus four paid annual commercial tiers (Starter, Professional, Business, and Enterprise Features Add-on). Asset Command Center is priced as an enterprise asset governance and intelligence module for standalone or suite deployment.

Contact Sales -> Product Details
Commercial routes are available with procurement support. During Stripe repricing refresh, contact licensing for immediate ordering and invoice/PO handling. Licence entitlement is issued through Keygen after successful commercial processing. Licensed under the Business Source License 1.1 (BSL) — converts to MIT on 14 February 2030.
