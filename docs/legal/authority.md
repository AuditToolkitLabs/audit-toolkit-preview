# Legal Authority

This page defines the legal authority boundary for AuditToolkit documentation.

## Canonical Source

The `legal/` section in AuditToolkit-Docs is the canonical legal authority for:

- End User License Agreement (EULA)
- Liability disclaimer and indemnity
- Legal notice references used by release bundles

Product repositories must not maintain legal policy as authoritative content.
They may include operational pointers only.

## Canonical Legal Documents

- [EULA](eula.md)
- [Liability Disclaimer and Indemnity](liability-disclaimer-and-indemnity.md)

## Repository Rule

When local product docs mention legal terms, they must link to central legal
authority pages in this section.
