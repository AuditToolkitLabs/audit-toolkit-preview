# Competitive Positioning

## Positioning Frame

AuditToolkit should be positioned as a self-hosted security and operations suite
with strong coverage in hardening/compliance execution and integrated reporting.

## Win Themes

- Self-hosted operational control for sensitive environments
- Broad script and audit execution footprint (where published)
- Package-first deployment model for enterprise operations
- Practical integration pathways through APIs and export contracts
- Central governance and documentation controls that strengthen trust

## Caution Themes

- Avoid absolute replacement claims against mature all-in-one enterprise VM/ASPM
  suites unless scope parity is proven in the current release.
- Use roadmap language for partial or in-progress capabilities.
- Keep competitive comparisons tied to verifiable product behavior.

## Safe Claim Pattern

Use:

- "Strong in self-hosted hardening/compliance and integrated reporting"
- "Designed for teams that need operational control and verifiable workflows"

Avoid:

- "Complete parity with all enterprise VM/ASPM workflows"
- "Drop-in replacement for every deployment model"

## Deal-Stage Guidance

Early stage:

- Lead with deployment control, auditability, and operational trust.

Mid stage:

- Demonstrate specific use cases with product-aligned proof points.

Late stage:

- Validate integration fit, governance requirements, and release-process
  compatibility.
