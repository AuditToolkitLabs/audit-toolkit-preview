# Ideal Customer Profiles

## ICP 1: Mid-Market Security Operations Team

Profile:

- 100 to 3000 servers/endpoints across mixed environments
- Lean SecOps team with compliance obligations
- Preference for self-hosted tools and direct operational control

Buying triggers:

- Inconsistent hardening evidence
- Manual reporting burden for audits
- Need for API-driven integration with existing tooling

Core message:
Unify audit evidence and operational action in a self-hosted platform that your
team can run and verify directly.

## ICP 2: Regulated Enterprise Infrastructure Team

Profile:

- Multi-team ownership across security, infra, and operations
- Formal change control and release governance requirements
- Requirement for package-first deployment and strong traceability

Buying triggers:

- Fragmented controls across legacy and modern systems
- Repeated compliance cycles requiring reproducible evidence
- Need to align remediation and reporting across domains

Core message:
Use an auditable, package-driven suite with governance-friendly workflows,
central documentation controls, and verifiable release evidence.

## ICP 3: Network Security And Exposure Team

Profile:

- Network-heavy environments with switch estate complexity
- Need for firmware/advisory/CVE correlation by vendor and platform
- Limited tolerance for blind spots in switch lifecycle risk

Buying triggers:

- Incomplete advisory normalization across vendors
- Manual CVE correlation effort
- Slow remediation prioritization due to incomplete exposure context

Core message:
Make switch exposure visible and actionable with advisory-aware enrichment and
operations-ready reporting.

## ICP 4: MSP And Multi-Tenant Service Operators

Profile:

- Managed customer environments with tenant boundaries
- Need for per-tenant entitlement and operational separation
- Strong demand for automation and repeatable runbooks

Buying triggers:

- Need for controlled multi-tenant execution boundaries
- Reporting and export consistency across customers
- Operational overhead from disconnected toolchains

Core message:
Scale managed operations with tenant-aware controls, integration-friendly
interfaces, and central policy alignment.
