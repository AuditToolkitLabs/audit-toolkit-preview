# Support Engagement Guide

## When to contact support

Contact support when the issue affects data freshness, product availability,
source access, or report integrity.

## Information to include

- Product version
- Current deployment type
- The affected vendor or module
- The exact error message or warning
- Recent configuration changes
- Time and frequency of the issue

## Useful evidence

- Job history
- Source status
- Health check output
- Relevant logs
- Screenshots of the affected view

## Support outcome

The goal of support is to identify whether the problem is configuration,
connectivity, source access, or a product defect.
