# Licensing and Legal

## Summary

This documentation is a customer-friendly summary of product use and support
expectations. It is not a legal substitute for the repository's legal terms.

## Use expectations

- Use the product within the agreed license and deployment scope.
- Do not share sensitive source credentials broadly.
- Review operational changes before applying them to production.

## Customer obligations

Customers are expected to provide the infrastructure, credentials, and access
paths needed for their chosen deployment and vendor source model.

## Support and contracts

If licensing, support, or commercial terms are needed, use the formal contract
and support channels rather than relying on the technical documentation.
