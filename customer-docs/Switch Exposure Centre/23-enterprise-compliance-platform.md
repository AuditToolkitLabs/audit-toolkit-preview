# Enterprise Compliance Platform

## Purpose

Switch Exposure Center can be used as part of a broader enterprise compliance
and vulnerability reporting process, especially where network infrastructure
needs to be tracked separately from general endpoint tooling.

## Enterprise usage themes

- Centralised switch exposure reporting
- Security review across multiple teams
- Remediation tracking by vendor or platform family
- Scheduled reporting for leadership or audit stakeholders
- Integration with wider governance or compliance workflows

## How to position the product

The product is best positioned as a focused operational system for switch and
adjacent network equipment rather than a universal risk-management platform.
It is strongest when used to make device inventory, advisory posture, and
remediation progress easy to review.
