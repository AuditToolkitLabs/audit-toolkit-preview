### Trial

New installations may activate a **14-day Trial** which grants
Professional-tier features. The trial:

- Requires email registration (machine-bound; reinstall does not reset
  the trial clock).
- Limits exports — reports are watermarked "EVALUATION ONLY".
- Automatically reverts to Community (25 servers) on expiry.
- Cannot be extended beyond one 7-day extension request.

### Advanced Operations Pack (add-on)

Available as an add-on to **Business** and **Enterprise** plans, the
**Advanced Operations Pack** unlocks:

- Advanced hypervisor audit modules (ESXi, Proxmox, Nutanix, KVM).
- CIS/compliance hypervisor audit packs.
- Hypervisor discovery and bulk operations.
- Hypervisor scheduling workflows.
- Optional remediation workflow guidance.

Support entitlement remains that of the underlying base plan.
Contact [License@audittoolkitlabs.com](mailto:License@audittoolkitlabs.com)
to add this pack to an existing Business or Enterprise subscription.
