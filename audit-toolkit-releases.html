Downloads & Releases Portal → Request Evaluation Access
Downloads are hosted on the AuditToolkit Labs Customer Release Portal (Microsoft SharePoint). Clicking the download link will open a dedicated releases page in your browser.
  • If the page opens directly — no sign-in is required. Select the version you need and download.
  • If prompted to sign in — sign in with any free Microsoft account (Outlook.com, Hotmail.com, or a work/school Microsoft 365 account). No paid licence is required to sign in.
  • If access is denied after signing in — contact us with your Microsoft account email address and we will add you as a guest to the portal within one business day.

Don’t have a Microsoft account? Create a free one here. Guest access is read-only and scoped to the Releases portal only.
