Licensing & Pricing

Audit Admin Toolkit

Core tier model: Community (free), Starter, Professional, and Business. Tiers are based on the number of managed servers, with the Audit Admin Toolkit positioned as the core reporting and security audit platform across the suite. Optional add-on packs are available alongside paid base tiers.

Contact Sales -> Product Details
Community tier is free with no expiry. Commercial licensing is required above 25 servers and for MSP/MSSP operation.
